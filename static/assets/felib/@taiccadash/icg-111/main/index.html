文化內容策進院

「2022沉浸式內容國際合資或合製支持計畫」申請案

切結書

茲保證本單位所提 （以下簡稱本案）及立切結書人 （即申請人）符合下列事項：

  1. 承諾所送所有申請文件、資料均無虛偽不實、隱匿之情事，且符合「2022沉浸式內容國際合資或合製支持計畫」作業要點規定。

  2. 承諾相同或類似之申請案內容未獲文化內容策進院、文化部及其所屬機關(構)、文化部單位預算下補（捐）助之相關單位支持。

  3. 承諾本案如獲文化內容策進院支持，將以申請者名義執行。

  4. 本案如獲文化內容策進院經費支持，承諾依該院核定之計畫書所載內容執行，且需符合「2022沉浸式內容國際合資或合製支持計畫」作業要點規定；計畫書有變更者，亦同。

  5. 本案如獲支持，承諾不會將獲支持資格或計畫書轉讓予他人。

  6. 擔保計畫書內容及依計畫書所辦理之各項工作，均無侵害他人權利或違反法律規定之情事。

  7. 本案如獲支持，承諾於計畫執行期間，將依文化內容策進院及文化部要求提供書面資料或出席會議說明，並依文化內容策進院之意見確實執行。

  8. 本案如獲支持，承諾將於文化內容策進院公告日起二個月內完成簽約。若逾期未完成簽約，承諾放棄經費支持受領資格及一切相關權益。

  9. 本案如獲支持，承諾於所完成之開發成果內容片首或片尾露出文化內容策進院LOGO，並列名該院為作品出品人之一。

  10. 本案如獲支持，承諾所完成之開發成果將臺灣列為出品國，且提供合資或合製之臺灣團隊應共同曝光之權益。

  11. 本案如獲支持，承諾於製作期間至結案後兩年內，配合提供進度說明予文化內容策進院，並依該院要求參與相關工作坊活動分享提案內容與製作經驗交換。

  12. 本案如獲支持，承諾同意文化內容策進院基於推廣臺灣文化內容產業之相關行銷活動為前提之情況下，依該院要求協助行銷推廣相關活動。

  13. 本案如獲支持，承諾其單位非屬大陸地區的人民、法人團體或其他機構、或非屬大陸地區人民來臺投資許可辦法第三條第二項所定之第三地區投資之公司。

  14. 本案之內部團隊成員與跨國合製案將依中國民國所得稅法及相關法令規定辦理所得申報扣繳及歸戶事宜，如有不實，願負一切法律責任。

Taiwan Creative Content Agency

2022 Immersive Content Grant for International Co-Funding or Co-Productions Program

Declaration Statement

The Applicant, , with the project named (hereinafter referred to as the “Project”) for 2022 Immersive Content Grant for International Co-Funding or Co-Productions Program, hereby warrants that:

  1. All documents and/or data submitted from the Applicant do not contain false information and/or conceal the facts, which conforms to the regulations of the “2022 Immersive Content Grant Application Guideline for the Immersive Content Grant for International Co-Funding or Co-Productions Program” (hereinafter referred to as the “Guideline”).

  2. The Applicant HAS NOT used the same or a similar Project to acquire any grant from Taiwan Creative Content Agency (TAICCA), Ministry of Culture of Taiwan (hereinafter referred to as the “MOC”), MOC’s affiliated agencies/institutions, and/or relevant agencies/institutions subsidized/donated by the MOC’s budget.

  3. Should the Applicant acquire the grant qualification in accordance with the Guideline (hereinafter referred to as “Grant Qualification”) from TAICCA, the Applicant would execute the Project without subcontracting it. 

  4. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, it would execute the Project in accordance with the proposal approved by TAICCA.

  5. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, it would not transfer the Grant Qualification or the proposal of the Project to a third party.

  6. The Applicant represents and warrants that neither the proposal of the Project nor any of the works performed or deliverables provided in accordance with the proposal does violate any rights of third parties and/or any laws.

  7. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, it would follow the instruction of TAICCA and/or MOC to provide written documents and/or attend meetings within the period of the execution of the Project. Furthermore, it would follow the opinions of TAICCA to execute the Project.

  8. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, it would sign a contract with TAICCA within two months from the date of announcement of the approved grant by TAICCA. Failing to fulfil the condition, the Applicant would give up any rights to Grant Qualification and any rights to it.

  9. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, the TAICCA’s logo would appear either at the beginning or in the tail-end credits of the work provided in accordance with the Project (hereinafter referred to as the “Work”), and the Work shall state TAICCA being one of the co-presenters.

  10. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, Taiwan would be listed as the presenting country of the Work; and the Taiwanese co-funding or co-production team would be entitled to the right to common exposure with the Applicant.

  11. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, it would provide a description of progress during the production period and until two years after the project completion date, and follow TAICCA’s directions to participate in relevant workshop activities to share the content of the project and exchange production experience.

  12. Should the Applicant acquire the Grant Qualification from TAICCA, it would follow TAICCA’s directions and assist TAICCA in preparing and/or executing the related activities, which is based on the goal of promoting Taiwan cultural content industry and with the cooperation with a marketing promotion plan for this goal.

  13. It is neither a natural person, legal person or other entity with the nationality of the People's Republic of China, nor does not constitute the entity/subject set out in Article 3 Section 2 of the Measures Governing Investment Permit to the People of Mainland Area and Its Rulings （大陸地區人民來臺投資許可辦法）.

  14. The Applicant and its team members would conduct tax withheld in accordance with the tax laws of Taiwan.

Personal Data Usage Statement

Based on Paragraph 1, Article 8 of the Personal Data Protection Act, Taiwan Creative Content Agency (TAICCA) would like to inform you about the following rights and requirements.


TAICCA takes your privacy and personal data autonomy very seriously. To ensure your data security, TAICCA abides by Paragraph 1, Article 8 of the Personal Data Protection Act (the “PDPA”) to inform you about your rights. With consent, TAICCA will collect, process, and use the personal data provided by you.

1. The purposes of the collection

TAICCA’s Immersive Content Grant online registration system (the “System”) collects your personal data for specific purposes, such as proposal evaluation, credentials verification, selection, data management, marketing campaign, statistics research, and System improvement. All personal data will be collected, processed, and used under the PDPA.

2. The categories of the personal data to be collected
  1. Basic information (including but not limited to name, ID number, company name, job title, address, and contact information)
  2. Personal features (including but not limited to personal photo, age, gender, and date of birth)
  3. Personal experiences (including but not limited to education background, work experiences, achievements, accolades, and other descriptions)
  4. Financial information (including but not limited to bank account name and number, credit history, and bounced check records)
  5. When you use the System, webpages will automatically collect data from browsing (including but not limited to your user name)
3. The time period, territory, recipients, and methods of which the personal data is used
  1. Time period: During aforementioned purposes exist, TAICCA operating years, or as related regulations require, whichever is longer.
  2. Territory: Within Republic of China (Taiwan) territory and beyond.
  3. Recipients: TAICCA and all necessary third parties and suppliers that fulfill aforementioned purposes.
  4. Methods: Collect, process, transfer, and use via electronic, written, phone, and other reasonable and legitimate methods.
4. Confidentiality and security

TAICCA implements rigorous employee protocols on your personal data access, and abides by regulatory requirements to protect your data security.

5. The data subject's rights under Article 3 of the PDPA and the methods for exercising such rights

After you use the System, you can make an inquiry of and review the stored data, or request data copies via email to TAICCA (grant.gm@taicca.tw). You can also request TAICCA to supplement, correct or erase the data. You can also demand the cessation of the collection, processing, or use of your personal data. If these requests impede certain purposes, TAICCA may limit or terminate your registrations. If you refuse to or fail to provide necessary or complete data in the System, it may lead to registration failures and impeding the execution of aforementioned purposes, which will affect your rights and interests. You may use the same email channel to object the marketing or promoting information from TAICCA.

6. Others

When you use the System, TAICCA will also collect your company data, including but not limited to credit history, balance sheets, income statements, and bounced check records. If you refuse to or fail to provide necessary or complete data in the System, it may lead to registration failures and impeding the execution of aforementioned purposes, which will infect your rights and interests.

個人資料使用聲明

文化內容策進院履行個人資料保護法第八條第一項告知義務內容告知書


文化內容策進院（下稱本院）十分重視您的隱私權及個人資料自主權。為維護您個人資料的安全性，本院謹遵循「個人資料保護法」（下稱個資法）第八條第一項規定，向您告知下列相關權益事項，俾取得您同意本院蒐集、處理及利用您所提供之個人資料：

1. 蒐集之特定目的：

本院之「沉浸式內容國際合資合製補助案」使用線上報名系統（下稱本系統）蒐集您（申請者）的個人資料，係為審查提案、評估及審查資格、評選、資料管理、聯繫、推廣行銷、統計研究、改善本系統等特定目的，進而在個資法規範下蒐集、處理及利用您所提供之個人資料。

2. 個人資料之類別：
  1. 個人基本資料（包括且不限於：姓名、身分證字號、公司名稱、職稱、住址、聯絡方式等）。
  2. 個人相關特徵（包括且不限於：個人照片、年齡、性別、出生年月日等）。
  3. 個人相關經驗資訊（包括且不限於：學經歷、過往成就、得獎紀錄、相關經驗描述等）。
  4. 個人財務資訊（包括且不限於：銀行帳號、戶名、徵信結果、無跳票紀錄等）。
  5. 當您使用本系統時，網頁會自動蒐集因瀏覽頁面而取得之資料（包括且不限於您的會員名稱）。
3. 個人資料利用之期間、地區、對象及方式 :
  1. 期間：上述特定目的存續期間、本院營運期間或相關法令規定須保存期間（取其長者）。
  2. 地區：中華民國境內、外。
  3. 對象：本院及為達成上述特定目的範圍內所必要之第三人、協力廠商。
  4. 方式：以電子、書面、電話等合理合法方式蒐集、處理、傳輸及利用。
4. 資料保密性與安全性：

本院對於所屬員工存取您的個人資料有嚴格的規範，並符合法規要求，以保護您個人資料的安全。

5. 依個資法第3條規定得行使之權利及方式：

您使用本系統後，得向本院以email方式（grant.gm@taicca.tw）請求查詢、閱覽所留存之資料或請求製給複製本，亦可請求本院補充、更正及刪除之，或要求本院停止蒐集、處理或利用您的個人資料。倘您為前述請求，致影響特定目的之執行，本院得限制或終止您的報名資格。若您拒絕提供或未完整提供本系統所需之資料，將可能導致無法報名成功及上述目的之執行，並進而影響您的權益。您亦得依上述email方式，通知本院您拒絕接受本院相關行銷或推廣資訊。

6. 其他：

本院將於您使用本系統時，一併蒐集您公司之相關資料（包括且不限於：徵信結果、資產負債表、損益表、無跳票紀錄等），若您拒絕提供或未完整提供，亦將可能導致無法報名成功及上述目的之執行，並進而影響您的權益。
